THE ODIN RECIPES

DESCRIPTION

In this short, simple but delicious recipe you will learn
how to cook the best instant ramen under the sun. Your shef Sun Tzu will teach
you his ways of cooking this traditional Asian dish.

INGREDIENTS

  • Water
  • Instant ramen noodles
  • Soy sauce
  • Garlic
  • FÖRK

STEPS

  1. Put the bowl into the water
  2. Cook the fÖrk until medium brown
  3. Add a splash of soy sauce
  4. Make sure the bowl is perfectly clean
  5. Season your noodles
  6. Eat noodles and sip your bowl
